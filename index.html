Why Surprise3D? 为什么需要Surprise3D？

Current 3D vision-language datasets let models take semantic shortcuts — they match object names in queries rather than truly reasoning about spatial relationships. We found that even strong multimodal models drop to near-zero accuracy on pure spatial reasoning tasks when object names are removed.

当前的3D视觉-语言数据集允许模型走语义捷径——它们匹配查询中的对象名称，而非真正推理空间关系。我们发现，当移除对象名称后，即使是强大的多模态模型在纯空间推理任务上的准确率也接近于零。

Surprise3D fixes this by crafting queries that describe spatial relationships without naming the target object, covering four distinct spatial reasoning skills with human-written queries, and providing the first standardized 3D Spatial Reasoning Segmentation (3D-SRS) benchmark.

Surprise3D 通过以下方式解决这一问题：构建不包含目标对象名称的空间关系描述查询，覆盖四种不同的空间推理技能的人工标注查询，并提供首个标准化的3D空间推理分割（3D-SRS）基准。

Spatial Reasoning Types 空间推理类型

Relative Position

相对位置

"Find the object behind the chair"
"找到椅子后面的物体"

Narrative Perspective

叙事视角

"Entering the door, with your back to the door, the object on your left side"
"进门后，背对门，你左手边的物体"

Parametric Perspective

参数视角

"At the perspective (3.00, -0.76, -1.24, 0.19, 0.025, 0.034, 0.00, -1.00), what's the closest object to sit?"
"在视角(3.00, -0.76, -1.24, ...)处，最近的可坐物体是什么？"

Absolute Distance

绝对距离

"A chair that is 2.63 meters away from the balcony door"
"距阳台门2.63米的椅子"

Data Analysis 数据分析

Annotation Pipeline 标注流水线

Surprise3D annotations are built through two complementary pipelines: a human spatial annotation process for high-quality spatial queries, and an automatic generation pipeline for scalable knowledge-based queries.

Surprise3D 的标注通过两条互补的流水线构建：用于高质量空间查询的人工空间标注流程，以及用于可扩展知识类查询的自动生成流水线。

1. Spatial Annotation (Human) 1. 空间标注（人工）

2. Automatic Pipeline (LLM + Human Verification) 2. 自动流水线（LLM + 人工验证）
